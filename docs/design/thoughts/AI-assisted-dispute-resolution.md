# AI Assisted Dispute Resolution

This is a placeholder to document an AI assisted dispute resolution process. 

- It should be handled by a designated service. 
- Access the rental history from the notifications
- Access the rental record. 
- It should have an escalation to the admin in the organization. 
- Every communications among the owner, the renter, the admin, the AI will be recorded. 
- May affect the rating if a rating system is implemented. 
- The organization may impose a dispute resolution policy for the AI to handle the dispute.
